FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our security services.

Are your security guards PSRA licensed?

Yes. All our security personnel are fully licensed and registered with the Private Security Regulatory Authority (PSRA).

Which counties do you operate in?

We operate in Nairobi, Machakos, Kajiado, Mombasa, Kilifi, Kisumu, Busia, and Marsabit Counties.

Do you provide emergency security deployment?

Yes. Our 24/7 operations centre can deploy rapid response teams for emergency security requirements.

Can you secure construction sites?

Yes. We specialise in site security, asset protection, and access control for construction and industrial sites.

Do you offer 24/7 guarding services?

Yes. All our manned guarding services operate around the clock, with supervisor support and incident reporting.

How quickly can services begin?

We can typically deploy within 24–48 hours after the security assessment and proposal approval.
