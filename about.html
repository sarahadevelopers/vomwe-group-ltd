Corporate History

Incorporated Under the Companies Act 2015

Vomwe Limited was duly incorporated and registered under the Companies Act 2015, Laws of Kenya. Having officially commenced operations in 2019, the firm has established a strict record of compliance, technical precision, and unwavering reliability within the private security sector.

Headquartered in Kitengela, we have grown from a localized operation into a fully licensed and authorized security force capable of protecting complex industrial infrastructures, high-profile events, and critical assets nation-wide.

Meet Our Managing Director

Visionary leadership driving security excellence across Kenya.

MD & Founder

Steve Odero

Managing Director
MSc Security Management PSRA Licensed 15+ Years Experience

Steve Odero is the founder and Managing Director of Vomwe Group, bringing over 15 years of distinguished experience in corporate security, risk management, and security operations across East Africa. He holds a Master of Science in Security Management from the University of Nairobi, with a specialization in Security Risk Analysis and Corporate Governance.

Before founding Vomwe Group in 2019, Steve served as Head of Security Operations at a leading multinational corporation, where he managed complex security infrastructures spanning multiple counties. His deep understanding of the regulatory landscape—including full compliance with the PSRA and the Companies Act 2015—ensures that Vomwe Group operates with the highest standards of professionalism and integrity.

"Security is not just about guarding property—it's about protecting livelihoods, preserving business continuity, and building trust. At Vomwe Group, we don't just provide security; we deliver peace of mind."
— Steve Odero, Managing Director
