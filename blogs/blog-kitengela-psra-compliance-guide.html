Why Kitengela Businesses Need PSRA-Compliant Security

The Private Security Regulatory Authority (PSRA) regulates all private security service providers in Kenya. Any company offering security services in Kitengela must be fully licensed and compliant. This means:

  • Valid PSRA License: All security companies must hold a valid license
  • Employee Vetting: All guards must undergo thorough background checks
  • Comprehensive Training: Formal training in security operations, first aid, and emergency response
  • Insurance Coverage: Proper liability and workers' compensation insurance
"Kitengela businesses that use PSRA-licensed security providers reduce their legal liability and operational risk significantly." — PSRA Compliance Guidelines, 2024

How to Verify a Security Company's PSRA Status

Before engaging any security company in Kitengela, local businesses should take these verification steps:

  • Request a copy of the PSRA license – Always ask to see the current license
  • Verify with PSRA – Confirm the license status directly through the PSRA portal
  • Check employee vetting certificates – Ensure all guards have valid compliance certificates
  • Review insurance documents – Validate adequate insurance coverage
